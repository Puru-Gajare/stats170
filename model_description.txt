### Slide: Housing Price Prediction Model

**Model Type:** Multiple Linear Regression (Ordinary Least Squares)

**Objective:** Predict the median house value for California districts based on demographic and geographical features.

**The Equation:**
MedianHouseValue = beta_0 + (beta_1 * MedianIncome) + (beta_2 * OceanEncoded) + (beta_3 * HousingAge) + (beta_4 * Households) + (beta_5 * Bedrooms) + (beta_6 * Population) + (beta_7 * TotalRooms) + epsilon

**Key Model Parameters (Estimated):**
- Intercept (beta_0): -49,516
- Coefficients:
  * Median Income: +$47,818 (Strongest Predictor)
  * Ocean Encoding: +$4,025
  * House Median Age: +$1,826
  * Households: +$113
  * Total Bedrooms: +$106
  * Population: -$32
  * Total Rooms: -$20

**Data Preparation:**
- Dropped missing values and duplicates.
- Encoded 'Ocean Proximity' into numerical categories.
- Removed Latitude/Longitude to avoid unstable coefficients while retaining proximity data.
- 80/20 Train/Test split for evaluation.

**Model Performance:**
- R^2 Score: 0.575 (Explains ~57.5% of the variance)
